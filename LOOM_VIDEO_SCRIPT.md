# Loom Video Demo Script - Dr. Maya Reynolds Website

## Video Duration: 3-5 minutes

---

## 🎬 Introduction (20 seconds)

**Opening:**
> "Hi Dr. Reynolds! I'm excited to show you the first draft of your new therapy website. I've designed this based on your Psychology Today profile to attract your ideal clients in Santa Monica. Let me walk you through the key features."

---

## 🖥️ Desktop Walkthrough (2 minutes)

### **Hero & About Sections** (30 seconds)
**What to show:** Scroll from top through hero and about sections

**What to say:**
> "Right at the top, your name and credentials establish trust immediately. The headline 'Find calm beyond the overwhelm' speaks directly to high-achieving clients who are struggling internally. The arched window design creates a warm, welcoming feel. The about section addresses their pain point - 'You deserve more than just pushing through' - which resonates with your target audience."

---

### **Specialties & Support** (30 seconds)
**What to show:** Show three specialty cards, scroll to support section

**What to say:**
> "Here are your three main focus areas: Anxiety & Panic, Trauma & PTSD, and Burnout & Stress - all from your profile. Each card uses relatable language instead of clinical jargon. The support section lists specific challenges like racing thoughts and perfectionism, helping visitors recognize themselves immediately."

---

### **Intro & Office Sections** (30 seconds)
**What to show:** Show intro section, scroll to office images

**What to say:**
> "The intro section highlights your credentials and modalities - CBT, EMDR, and mindfulness. The office section shows your Santa Monica space with three images, helping clients visualize their visit. We included your address and note that you offer both in-person and telehealth options for California residents."

---

### **FAQ, Background & Footer** (30 seconds)
**What to show:** Click one FAQ, show accordion background, scroll to footer

**What to say:**
> "The FAQ addresses common questions in plain language. Your professional background is organized into expandable categories establishing expertise without overwhelming visitors. The footer has all your contact info, hours, and important links."

---

## 📱 Mobile Walkthrough (45 seconds)

**What to show:** Resize to mobile view, scroll through sections

**What to say:**
> "Now let's look at mobile, since most people search for therapists on their phone. Everything automatically adapts - navigation condenses, sections stack vertically, images resize perfectly. The arched window design looks great on mobile too. Notice the scroll animations as you move down - each section smoothly fades in, keeping visitors engaged."

---

## 🎨 Design Choices & Benefits (1 minute)

**What to say:**
> "Quick overview of key design decisions: We chose terracotta, sage, and cream colors - warm and calming, different from typical therapy websites. The arched window design symbolizes 'looking into' a safe space. All copy speaks directly to high-achieving adults struggling internally, avoiding clinical jargon."

> "The benefits: This targets your exact client audience, builds trust with clear credentials, includes 'Santa Monica' for local search optimization, works beautifully on mobile, and has clear calls-to-action throughout. Everything is based on your actual profile."

---

## 🎬 Closing (15 seconds)

**What to say:**
> "So that's your new website! It's designed to attract your ideal clients and make it easy for them to reach out. I'd love to hear your feedback on what resonates and any changes you'd like. Thanks for watching!"

---

## ✅ Video Checklist

- [ ] Video is 5-10 minutes long
- [ ] Presented as client demo to Dr. Maya Reynolds
- [ ] Includes desktop walkthrough of all sections
- [ ] Includes mobile walkthrough
- [ ] Explains design choices in non-technical terms
- [ ] Mentions how profile information was used
- [ ] Focuses on benefits and client experience
- [ ] Clear, confident communication
- [ ] No technical jargon (no mentions of React, Next.js, Tailwind, etc.)
- [ ] Professional but warm tone
- [ ] Ends with call for feedback

---

## 📝 Recording Tips

1. **Speak slowly and clearly** - pause between sections
2. **Show, don't just tell** - interact with the website as you describe it
3. **Smile while talking** - it comes through in your voice
4. **Use the cursor to point** at specific elements you're discussing
5. **Rehearse once** before recording to smooth out any awkward pauses
6. **Keep energy up** throughout the video
7. **Address Dr. Reynolds directly** as if she's watching

---

## 🎥 Technical Setup

- Use Loom desktop app for best quality
- Set to 1080p resolution
- Enable microphone and system audio
- Full screen browser on desktop view
- Have mobile device or responsive view ready
- Close unnecessary tabs/applications
- Use incognito/private browsing to avoid personal bookmarks showing
